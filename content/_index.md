# Welcome to Imbereheza 

Imbereheza roughly translates as progress/development in Kinyarwanda, the native language of Rwanda. 

My name is Jack and I'm a Kigali based economist passionate about economics, politics, and sports. This website is currently functioning as a place to hold some personal posts and photos, but I’m hoping to write some longer content about economics and politics as I find the time to do so. My long-term aim is to help contribute to the progress of Rwanda both through my writing and my work here.  

You can find my Twitter [here](https://twitter.com/jackd1801). If you're in Kigali or just want to say hi please do drop me a message there.
